Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products 100% organic?

Yes — our products are certified organic where certification applies and are grown without harmful synthetic pesticides or chemicals.

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes — weekly delivery is available to select areas. Check the Services page for current delivery areas and schedules.

Can I place bulk orders?

Yes — we cater to bulk orders for households, businesses and events. Please contact us via the Contact page for quotes and availability.

Where can I buy your products?

Our products are available via our online shop, in selected stores, and at local farmers’ markets.
