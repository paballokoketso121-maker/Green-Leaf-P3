About Greenleaf Organic

At Greenleaf Organic we believe in nourishing people and protecting the planet. Our mission is to provide high-quality, pesticide-free, eco-friendly food products that promote healthy living and sustainable farming.

Our Values

  • Sustainability: Supporting eco-friendly farming practices.
  • Health: Providing natural, chemical-free, nutrient-rich products.
  • Community: Building strong relationships with local farmers.
  • Transparency: Honesty about our products and practices.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 in Johannesburg, Greenleaf Organic began as a small family-run stall at farmers’ markets. Today we supply a wide range of organic produce, dairy alternatives, and healthy snacks, partnering with farms across South Africa.

Our Mission in Practice

From supporting regenerative farming to ensuring fair pay for producers, every product we sell reflects our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Where We Are

Head office and main farm operation: Johannesburg. We also work with partner farms across the Western Cape and KwaZulu-Natal.
